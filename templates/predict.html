How to Use the Water Quality Prediction Tool

Ensuring access to clean and safe water is essential for health and well-being. Our Water Quality Prediction Tool helps you determine whether water is safe for human consumption based on key parameters.
Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Input Water Quality Parameters

Enter numerical values for each water quality component:

  • pH (Range: 0-14)
  • Nitrate, Turbidity, Copper, Chlorine
  • Total Dissolved Solids, Iron, Chloride, Fluoride
  • Odor, Manganese

Step 2: Submit the Data for Prediction

After filling in all required fields, click the "Predict Now" button to process the data.

Step 3: Review the Prediction Result

The system will display a pop-up message:

  • ✅ Safe: The water is safe for use.
  • ❌ Unsafe: The water is unsafe and requires treatment.
